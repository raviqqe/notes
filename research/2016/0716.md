## None-1 attention for one-character words

In my `nn` module on TensorFlow,
attention for one-character words can sometimes be a little lower than 1.
This is caused by adapting calculation of attention to zero-character words.
To do so, we need to add machine epsilon to the denominator of softmax.
Then, the result values may change slightly
depending on values of the original denominators.
